- **Master's Thesis:** "[Leveraging Gemini Pro and BERT for Automated IMRaD Classification: A Novel Dataset and SaaS Platform](https://github.com/stormsidali2001/graduation_IMRAD_introduction_analysis_SaaS)". Architected a scalable 7-node distributed system (Next.js, FastAPI, TF Serving, Express.js, Eureka) to automate rhetorical classification of semantic moves within scientific paper introductions.
- **Key Contributions:** Engineered a 3-phase Gemini Pro pipeline to synthesize and validate a custom 169k-sentence dataset, boosting baseline accuracy from 44.61% to >94% (Peak F1: 98.21%) by fine-tuning 4 hierarchical BERT models.
